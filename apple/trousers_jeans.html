When wearing jeans or pants, make sure the top extends just below your hipbone to draw attention away from your midriff. Skinnys and tapered styles are tricky on your Apple body shape because they'll accentuate your thin legs and make you look bigger on top. A slight flare adds balance.

  • Flare and wide leg
  • Bootcut
  • Straight cut pants worn with a pair of eye-catching shoes
  • Normal to high rise with a flat front
  • Flat fronted trouser styles with some stretch, a structured wide waistband, and a higher rise are fab in straight and bootcut styles
  • Pair of cargo pocket pants as long as the fronts are flat
  • Skinnies can work, especially with flats or tucked into tall boots. Cropped skinnies are fine with a heel, or with flats if you stick to low-contrast dressing
  • Slim-fit boyfriend jeans, relaxed skinnies, or fluid chinos with rolled hems are great
